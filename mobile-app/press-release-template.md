# LusoTown Launches Revolutionary Mobile App Connecting Portuguese-Speaking Community Across the United Kingdom

## New platform brings together 1,500+ Portuguese speakers from Portugal, Brazil, Cape Verde, Angola, and all Lusophone nations living in the UK

**London, UK** - [Launch Date] - LusoTown, the UK's first comprehensive Portuguese-speaking community platform, today announced the official launch of its mobile application for iOS and Android devices. The app addresses the growing need for authentic cultural connections among the 1,500+ Portuguese speakers living across the United Kingdom, from London to Edinburgh, Manchester to Cardiff.

### Authentic Portuguese Community in Your Pocket

LusoTown's mobile app offers a unique digital space where Portuguese speakers from all Lusophone nations - including Portugal, Brazil, Cape Verde, Angola, Mozambique, Guinea-Bissau, São Tomé and Príncipe, and East Timor - can discover their cultural community in the UK.

### Key Features Celebrating Portuguese Culture

**Authentic Portuguese Events:** Discover Fado nights, Santos Populares celebrations, Portugal Day festivities, and traditional cultural workshops across the UK.

**Cultural Compatibility Matching:** AI-powered matching system that connects people based on shared Portuguese interests, heritage, and cultural values.

**Portuguese Business Directory:** Access to 200+ verified Portuguese-owned businesses, from traditional bakeries to modern tech startups, with exclusive member discounts.

**University Partnerships:** Official partnerships with 8 leading UK universities including UCL, King's College, Imperial College, LSE, Oxford, and Cambridge, supporting 1,000+ Portuguese-speaking students.

### Strategic Partnerships with Portuguese Cultural Institutions

LusoTown has established partnerships with prominent Portuguese cultural organizations:

- **Instituto Camões Centre London**: Official Portuguese government cultural institution
- **Centro Cultural Português de Londres**: Community-based cultural center
- **Casa do Brasil in London**: Premium Brazilian cultural institution

### Flexible Membership for Every Portuguese Family

The app offers multiple membership tiers designed for Portuguese community needs:

- **Free Tier**: Access to community events and basic matching
- **Community Member** (£15.99/month): Unlimited connections and exclusive Portuguese business discounts
- **Cultural Ambassador** (£29.99/month): Premium events and monthly Portuguese wine delivery
- **Família Plan** (£39.99/month): Perfect for Portuguese families

### Availability and Download

The LusoTown mobile app is now available for free download:
- **iOS**: Available on the Apple App Store
- **Android**: Available on Google Play Store
